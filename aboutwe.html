Excellence in Classic and Legendary Cars

VINTAGE is a company that stands out as a reference in the world of classic and legendary cars, recognized for its passion and expertise in the field of collecting extraordinary vintage vehicles. Under the visionary leadership of Pablo Alexandre de Vasconcelos, VINTAGE has established itself as one of the pioneers and most respected companies in this segment, attracting enthusiasts, collectors, and investors from around the world.

A Journey of Excellence and Passion

Since its founding, VINTAGE has been dedicated to rescuing and preserving automotive history, bringing to life iconic models that defined eras and styles. Each VINTAGE car is more than just a vehicle; it is a work of art on wheels, carefully restored and maintained to preserve its authenticity and legacy. The company boasts an impressive collection that ranges from elegant European roadsters of the 1950s to powerful American muscle cars of the 1970s, along with rare gems that captivate with their unique stories and design.

Visionary Leadership

Pablo Alexandre de Vasconcelos, the leader behind VINTAGE, has been passionate about classic cars since a young age and is deeply knowledgeable about the collectibles market. His dedication, strategic vision, and keen sense for identifying vehicles with significant historical and cultural value have been crucial to the company’s growth. Under his leadership, VINTAGE not only collects but also educates the public on the importance of preserving automotive heritage, promoting events, exhibitions, and gatherings that celebrate the passion for classic car culture.

Commitment to the Future

With an eye on the future, VINTAGE continues to expand its reach, exploring new opportunities in the global market and investing in technologies that respect the environment without compromising the authenticity of the vehicles. The company remains steadfast in its mission to preserve automotive history for future generations, ensuring that legends on four wheels continue to inspire and captivate.
